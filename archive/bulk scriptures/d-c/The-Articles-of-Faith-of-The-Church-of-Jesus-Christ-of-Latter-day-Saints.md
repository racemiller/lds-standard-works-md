# The Articles of Faith of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Chapter 1

1 We [abelieve](#note1a) in [bGod](#note1b), the Eternal Father, and in His [cSon](#note1c), Jesus Christ, and in the [dHoly Ghost](#note1d).

2 We believe that men will be [apunished](#note2a) for their [bown](#note2b) sins, and not for [cAdam’s](#note2c) transgression.

3 We believe that through the [aAtonement](#note3a) of Christ, all [bmankind](#note3b) may be [csaved](#note3c), by obedience to the laws and ordinances of the Gospel.

4 We believe that the first principles and [aordinances](#note4a) of the Gospel are: first, [bFaith](#note4b) in the Lord Jesus Christ; second, [cRepentance](#note4c); third, [dBaptism](#note4d) by [eimmersion](#note4e) for the [fremission](#note4f) of sins; fourth, Laying on of [ghands](#note4g) for the [hgift](#note4h) of the Holy Ghost.

5 We believe that a man must be [acalled](#note5a) of God, by [bprophecy](#note5b), and by the laying on of [chands](#note5c) by those who are in [dauthority](#note5d), to [epreach](#note5e) the Gospel and administer in the [fordinances](#note5f) thereof.

6 We believe in the same [aorganization](#note6a) that existed in the Primitive Church, namely, apostles, [bprophets](#note6b), [cpastors](#note6c), [dteachers](#note6d), [eevangelists](#note6e), and so forth.

7 We believe in the [agift](#note7a) of [btongues](#note7b), [cprophecy](#note7c), [drevelation](#note7d), [evisions](#note7e), [fhealing](#note7f), [ginterpretation](#note7g) of tongues, and so forth.

8 We believe the [aBible](#note8a) to be the [bword](#note8b) of God as far as it is translated [ccorrectly](#note8c); we also believe the [dBook of Mormon](#note8d) to be the word of God.

9 We believe all that God has [arevealed](#note9a), all that He does now reveal, and we believe that He will yet [breveal](#note9b) many great and important things pertaining to the Kingdom of God.

10 We believe in the literal [agathering](#note10a) of Israel and in the restoration of the [bTen Tribes](#note10b); that [cZion](#note10c) (the New Jerusalem) will be built upon the American continent; that Christ will [dreign](#note10d) personally upon the earth; and, that the earth will be [erenewed](#note10e) and receive its [fparadisiacal](#note10f) [gglory](#note10g).

11 We claim the [aprivilege](#note11a) of worshiping Almighty God according to the [bdictates](#note11b) of our own [cconscience](#note11c), and allow all men the same privilege, let them [dworship](#note11d) how, where, or what they may.

12 We believe in being [asubject](#note12a) to [bkings](#note12b), presidents, rulers, and magistrates, in [cobeying](#note12c), honoring, and sustaining the [dlaw](#note12d).

13 [aWe](#note13a) believe in being [bhonest](#note13b), true, [cchaste](#note13c), [dbenevolent](#note13d), virtuous, and in doing [egood](#note13e) to all men; indeed, we may say that we follow the admonition of Paul—We believe all things, we [fhope](#note13f) all things, we have endured many things, and hope to be able to [gendure](#note13g) all things. If there is anything [hvirtuous](#note13h), [ilovely](#note13i), or of good report or praiseworthy, we seek after these things.

Joseph Smith.
