# Abraham, Facsimile 3

Abraham, Facsimile 3 — Fig. 1. Abraham sitting upon Pharaoh’s throne, by the politeness of the king, with a crown upon his head, representing the Priesthood, as emblematical of the grand Presidency in Heaven; with the scepter of justice and judgment in his hand.

Abraham, Facsimile 3 — Fig. 2. King Pharaoh, whose name is given in the characters above his head.

Abraham, Facsimile 3 — Fig. 3. Signifies Abraham in Egypt as given also in Figure 10 of Facsimile No. 1.

Abraham, Facsimile 3 — Fig. 4. Prince of Pharaoh, King of Egypt, as written above the hand.

Abraham, Facsimile 3 — Fig. 5. Shulem, one of the king’s principal waiters, as represented by the characters above his hand.

Abraham, Facsimile 3 — Fig. 6. Olimlah, a slave belonging to the prince.
