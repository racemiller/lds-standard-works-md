# Statistical Report, 2007

**Presented by Elder F. Michael Watson** — April 2008 General Conference

Brothers and sisters, the First Presidency has issued the following report concerning the growth and activity of the Church as of December 31, 2007.

Stakes

2,790

Missions

348

Districts

618

Wards and Branches

27,827

Total Church Membership

13,193,999

Increase in Children of Record

93,698

Converts Baptized

279,218

Full-Time Missionaries

52,686

Temples Rededicated

1

(Nuku‘alofa Tonga)

Number of Temples Currently in Operation

124
