# Statistical Report 1994

**Presented by F. Michael Watson** — April 1995 General Conference

For the information of the members of the Church, the First Presidency has issued the following statistical report concerning the growth and status of the Church as of December 31, 1994. The statistics are based on 1994 reports available prior to this conference.

Stakes

2,008

Districts

709

Missions

303

Wards and branches

21,774

Nations and territories with organized wards or branches

156

Total membership

9,024,569

Eight-year-old children of record baptized during 1994

72,538

Converts baptized during 1994

300,730

Full-time missionaries

47,311

President Ezra Taft Benson, thirteenth President of the Church, at age 94, on May 30, 1994; President Howard W. Sill, emeritus General Authority; Eudora Widtsoe Durham, widow of Elder G. Homer Durham, former member of the Presidency of the Seventy; Weston Eugene Hamilton, member of the General Church Finance Committee; Oakley S. Evans, former president of the Tabernacle Choir.
