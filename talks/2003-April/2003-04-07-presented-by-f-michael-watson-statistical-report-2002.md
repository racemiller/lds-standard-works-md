# Statistical Report, 2002

**Presented by F. Michael Watson** — April 2003 General Conference

Brothers and sisters, the First Presidency has issued the following report concerning the growth and status of the Church as of December 31, 2002:

Missions

335

Stakes

2,602

Districts

641

Wards and Branches

26,143

Total Church Membership

11,721,548

Increase in Children of Record

81,132

Converts Baptized

283,138

Number of Full-Time Missionaries

61,638

Temples Dedicated during 2002

7

(Snowflake Arizona, Lubbock Texas, Monterrey México, Campinas Brazil, Asunción Paraguay, Nauvoo Illinois, and The Hague Netherlands)

Temples Rededicated during 2002

2

(Freiberg Germany and Monticello Utah)

Number of Temples Currently in Operation

114
